BioEngineer and Data Scientist

(THIS PAGE IS A WORK IN PROGRESS) I graduated from the University of Edinburgh with a PhD in Bioengineering. My thesis was on using Raman spectroscopy to probe the autophagy response in mammalian cells and using machine learning to create predictive models for this process. My academic focus is on using AI and machine learning to better understand biological data in research and healthcare settings.
